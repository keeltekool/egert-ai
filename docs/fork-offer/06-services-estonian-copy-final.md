## Tone notes

Use these wording choices consistently:

- `AI` stays as `AI`, not `tehisintellekt`, because the current page is aimed at practical business and builder audiences.
- `AI-native` stays `AI-native`. It is a positioning phrase, not a generic Estonian sentence.
- In the main marketing copy, prefer `päriskasutus`, `kasutusse viidud`, `juhtpaneel` and `piiritleda` over heavier English-product slang.
- Technical names and product-specific terms can stay in English where they add precision: `SaaS`, `full-stack`, `Claude Code`, `GitHub Actions`, `OAuth`, `FastAPI`, `Render`.
- Keep the proof-section details slightly more technical than the hero and offering copy. The hero sells clearly; the proof section earns credibility.
