# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, collaborators, potential clients, and the broader AI/indie builder community. They arrive with curiosity — "who is this person who shipped 45 apps in a year?" — and leave with conviction that this builder has real depth: production databases, payment flows, scraping pipelines, NLP engines, not just toy demos.

## Product Purpose
Personal builder portfolio and CV page. Establishes Egert Väinaste as a product manager who learned to ship production software with Claude Code at an unreasonable pace. The page IS the proof — its craft, density of real detail, and design quality should mirror the quality of the work it describes.

## Brand Personality
Confident, substantive, editorial. The work speaks. No hype, no flash — earned authority through real shipped systems. More like a well-designed case study than a template portfolio.

## Anti-references
- AI-template portfolios: cream bg + copper accent + identical card grids + section eyebrows. The exact pattern this page currently uses.
- Dark SaaS hero pages with glow blobs, gradient text, floating UI mockups.
- Generic "AI builder" sites that list buzzwords instead of showing real work.
- Any design where someone could say "AI made that" without doubt.

## Design Principles
1. **Show, don't tell** — real project details, real technical depth, real numbers. Not buzzwords.
2. **Earned confidence** — the portfolio speaks for itself. No hype copy needed.
3. **Substance is the style** — the density and quality of real work IS the visual interest. Don't decorate emptiness.
4. **Practice what you preach** — a builder's page should feel built, not generated.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA minimum. Body text ≥4.5:1 contrast. Reduced motion alternatives for all animation. Semantic HTML. Keyboard navigable.
